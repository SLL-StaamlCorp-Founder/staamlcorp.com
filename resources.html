FAQ for licensing counsel

Chain of title

U.S. Patent Application No. 19/640,793 was filed in the inventor's name (Stanley Lee Linton). Assignment to STAAML Corp. is in process; counsel may request the current USPTO recordation status from legal@staamlcorp.com.

Prosecution status

Track One Prioritized Examination. The application has not yet issued; claims may be amended during prosecution. Provisional rights under 35 U.S.C. §154(d) do not attach until publication and actual notice with substantively identical claims at issuance.

Claim mapping

Element-by-element claim charts mapping the independent claims to specific products or architectures are available under a mutual NDA. We do not maintain a public list of suspected implementers and do not send unsolicited threat letters.

Licensing posture

Fair, reasonable, and non-discriminatory terms. No-injunction commitment for good-faith negotiators. See the Licensing Policy for the public statement.

Standards-essential disposition

If any issued claims are determined to be essential to a published industry standard, StaamlCorp will license those claims on FRAND terms consistent with the relevant standards body's policy.

Outside counsel of record

Available to qualified counterparties on request to legal@staamlcorp.com.

Benchmark methodology

Performance numbers cited in earlier versions of this site (selective validation throughput, cache preservation, bandwidth reduction, and detection rate) will be republished alongside a methodology whitepaper describing test corpora, hardware, and statistical procedure. Until that document is published, those figures are withheld.
