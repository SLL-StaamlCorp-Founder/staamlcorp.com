Compliance & Certifications

StaamlCorp is committed to the highest standards of security, privacy, accessibility, and operational excellence.

Our Commitment to Compliance

As a cybersecurity intellectual property company, StaamlCorp holds itself to rigorous standards across security, privacy, quality, and accessibility. We are actively pursuing alignment with 12 internationally recognized certification frameworks to ensure our operations, website, and technology meet or exceed industry best practices.

Patented Security Technology

StaamlCorp's website and infrastructure are protected by our own patent-pending Temporal Session Bridging (TSB) technology (U.S. App. No. 19/640,793). TSB addresses cached executable persistence across security policy transitions, providing a fundamental security layer that goes beyond conventional approaches. This technology underpins our commitment to practicing what we preach in cybersecurity.

Certification Standards

ISO/IEC 27001

Information Security Management

We implement and maintain an Information Security Management System (ISMS) aligned with ISO 27001, establishing systematic policies, procedures, and controls to manage and protect information assets against threats.

ISO/IEC 27017

Cloud Security Management

Our cloud-hosted services follow ISO 27017 guidelines with cloud-specific security controls, shared responsibility definitions, and enhanced protections for cloud-based data and workloads.

ISO/IEC 27018

Personal Data in Cloud

We adhere to ISO 27018 principles for protecting personally identifiable information (PII) in cloud environments, ensuring transparency, consent-based processing, and strict data handling procedures.

SOC 2 Type 2

Trust Services Criteria

Our operations are aligned with SOC 2 Type 2 requirements across security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy trust services criteria, with continuous monitoring and evidence collection.

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management

StaamlCorp follows ISO 9001 quality management principles including customer focus, leadership engagement, process approach, evidence-based decision making, and continuous improvement across all operations.

ISO/IEC 27701:2019

Privacy Information Management

We extend our ISMS with a Privacy Information Management System (PIMS) aligned with ISO 27701, providing a structured framework for managing personal data in compliance with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

SOC 2 + HIPAA Type 2

Healthcare Data Protection

Our controls framework addresses HIPAA requirements alongside SOC 2, ensuring that any protected health information (PHI) is handled with appropriate administrative, physical, and technical safeguards.

SOC 1 (SSAE & ISAE 3402 — Type 2)

Financial Reporting Controls

We maintain internal controls relevant to financial reporting in alignment with SOC 1 standards, providing assurance over the processes and systems that may impact our clients' financial statements.

WCAG 2.2 Level AA

Web Content Accessibility

Our website is designed and developed to conform with WCAG 2.2 Level AA success criteria, ensuring content is perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for all users, including those with disabilities. See our Accessibility Statement.

ISO 22301 — BCMS

Business Continuity Management

StaamlCorp maintains a Business Continuity Management System aligned with ISO 22301, ensuring we can continue delivering critical services during and after disruptive events through planning, testing, and recovery procedures.

GoBD

German Compliance (Bookkeeping & Records)

We comply with GoBD (Grundsatze zur ordnungsmassigen Fuhrung und Aufbewahrung von Buchern, Aufzeichnungen und Unterlagen in elektronischer Form) requirements for proper management and retention of electronic business records, ensuring traceability, immutability, and auditability.

CST

Communications, Space & Technology

StaamlCorp aligns with CST (Communications, Space & Technology) regulatory frameworks governing secure communications infrastructure, ensuring our cybersecurity technology and operations meet sector-specific compliance requirements.

Data Handling Practices

StaamlCorp collects minimal data necessary for our operations. The data we handle includes:

  • Contact form submissions: Name, email, company, and message content — used solely to respond to inquiries.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Email addresses — used only to send opted-in communications.
  • Risk assessment responses: Assessment answers and results — used to provide security evaluation feedback.

All data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable data protection regulations. We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties.

Security Measures

Our security posture includes:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Access controls and least-privilege principles
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability monitoring
  • Incident response planning and business continuity procedures
  • Employee security awareness training
  • Our proprietary TSB technology (U.S. App. No. 19/640,793) providing protection against cached executable persistence vulnerabilities
